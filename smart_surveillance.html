Smart surveillance

Public security is one of the pressing concern of an increasingly urbanizing world. Governments and city corporations have understood it's importance. After the infamous attack on Mumbai in 2008 Mumbai has installed around 6000 CCTVs across the city. In 2017, Delhi Government signed to install 1.4 Lakh CCTVs in Delhi. The number of CCTVs have ever since then. Manually monitoring these CCTVs have become nearly impossible. The data generated by these CCTVs is humongous. This makes even crime investigation an inefficient and painstaking. Advaced AI Technology Laboratory - Advait Labs uses cutting edge research to improve the active surveillance. We automate the monitoring using modern AI techniques. This will significanty reduce the manpower requirement for active monitoring. At the same time the the algorithms analyze huge volumes of data and significantly accelerate crime investigation. Real time monitoring can significantly help in preventing crimes, improving public safety, accelerating investigation activities, suspect monitoring, crowd and traffic flow and control. Hence improving our quality of life.
